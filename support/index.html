Cuddlebrook Support

Need help with your rescue? We would be glad to hear from you.

Contact

Email cuddlebrookapp@gmail.com. Please describe what happened and, if possible, include your device model, iOS version, and the screen you were using. Do not send passwords or other sensitive information.

Game saves

Cuddlebrook saves progress on your device and uses your private iCloud key-value storage on iOS for continuity between devices signed in to the same Apple account. The developer cannot view or recover that private save.

Start over

To erase the current rescue, open Settings, choose Reset game, and confirm Reset everything. This cannot be undone.

Privacy

Read the Cuddlebrook privacy policy for details about local storage, iCloud, sharing, retention, and deletion.
