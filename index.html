Cuddlebrook privacy policy

Effective date: July 16, 2026
Developer: Cuddlebrook
Contact: cuddlebrookapp@gmail.com

This policy explains how Cuddlebrook handles information when you play the game.

Information Cuddlebrook handles

Cuddlebrook does not require an account and does not ask for an email address, phone number, mailing address, precise location, contacts, microphone, or camera access.

The game stores the information needed to preserve your rescue, including:

  • game progress, animals, adoptions, rooms, items, achievements, and settings;
  • the player name, rescue name, and pet names entered in the game; and
  • photos and keepsakes created inside the game.

This information is used only to provide the game's features.

Where game information is stored

Game information is stored locally on your device. On iOS, Cuddlebrook also uses Apple's private iCloud key-value storage to sync the game save between devices signed in to the same Apple account. Cuddlebrook does not operate a server for the game and cannot view or retrieve your local or iCloud game save.

Apple's handling of iCloud information is governed by Apple's own privacy terms and account controls.

Analytics, advertising, and tracking

Cuddlebrook does not include advertising, third-party analytics, cross-app tracking, or behavioral profiling. It does not sell personal information.

Photos and sharing

Cuddlebrook can create decorative images from game content on your device. The app saves or shares an image only when you choose the Save / share action. If you choose another app or service from the system share sheet, that destination handles the image under its own privacy policy.

Children

Cuddlebrook is designed so that play does not require an online account, public profile, free-form public posting, advertising, or disclosure of contact information. The game does not knowingly collect personal information from children or other players through a developer-operated service.

Retention and deletion

The game save remains on the device and in the player's private iCloud key-value store while it is being used for game continuity. To erase the current rescue, open Settings in Cuddlebrook, choose Reset game, and confirm Reset everything. This replaces the saved rescue with a new-game state and syncs that replacement through the same iCloud save mechanism.

You can also manage the app and iCloud through the controls provided by Apple on your device. Because Cuddlebrook cannot access your private game save, the developer cannot retrieve or delete that save on your behalf.

Security

Cuddlebrook limits game storage to the device and Apple's iCloud key-value service and does not operate a separate game-data server. No storage method is guaranteed to be perfectly secure, but the app minimizes exposure by avoiding unnecessary collection and transmission.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated if Cuddlebrook's features or data practices change. The effective date above will be updated when a revised policy is published.

Questions about this policy can be sent to cuddlebrookapp@gmail.com.
